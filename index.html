Project Concept

This project explores psychological tension, violence, and identity through experimental sound.

By combining necrotrap, horrorcore, techno, hardstyle, jumpstyle, and ambient music, I aimed to push beyond conventional genre boundaries.

Research & Influences

I analysed how distortion, repetition, and atmosphere are used within underground music scenes.

This research informed my use of dissonance, silence, and aggressive rhythm structures.

Development Process

I experimented with BPM ranges, distorted bass, and rhythmic structures inspired by hardstyle and techno.

I refined balance between clarity and intensity.
